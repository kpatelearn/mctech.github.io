About secret swims

established in 2022

Secret swims was founded in Tenarvia by Ella Ronaldson, it is a secret swimming hole just out of the Bavanahs and its only a 15 minute walk!. Theres so many things to do, cliff jumping, swim, sunbathe, etc. We do luckily have a cafe right next door, where everything is affordable and freshly made, using only the freshest products. To see more of our menu click the menu button in the top right hand corner to see more about the cafe!. If you would like to contact us about any further information, the information button is in the top right corner next to the menu button.
